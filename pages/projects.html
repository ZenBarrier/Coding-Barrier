FaceBlr

A Chrome extension that uses jQuery to manipulate DOM on Facebook.com to blur selected people's posts and comments. The AngularJS framework was used to create a dynamic extension popup page and Bootstrap was used to quickly and easily design a user friendly interface. (2015)

Wear Battery Full

Successfully developed an Android Wear app that is meant to inform users if their smart watch has completed charging. Developing this app has given me experience working with Android Studio and java, launching an app into the Google Play Store, and the basics of creating a mobile and wearable app.
